COVID-19 Safety Measures

In accordance with official guidelines by WHO

One of our highest priorities is the health, safety and security of our guests, team members and business partners.

Regular Temperature Checks

We regularly check our staff's Body Temperature and Oxygen Saturation level.

Double Dose Vaccinated Staff

All of our staff are vaccinated with both doses

Masked Staff

Our staff ensures masking all the time

Social Distancing

Our staff follows social Distancing

Proper Sanitization of rooms

We sanitize all our rooms regularly.

Ambulance

A cardio ambulance is ready 24*7 for our staff and guests.

Washing Hands

Our staff wash their hands regularly.

The health, safety, and security of our guests, team members, and business partners is one of our top objectives. COVID-19 has profoundly altered our way of life, and we're adapting our daily operations to accommodate the new normal. As a result, we assessed our existing health and safety systems and designed a new safety policy with the help of a team of specialists. From check-in to check-out, our comprehensive cleaning and disinfection routine is meant to ensure your peace and safety.
PCR Testing
Atithya Hotels can direct guests to a readily accessible and reasonable PCR testing site to ease the return of travel. Additionally, several hotels will provide on-site PCR testing for groups or individuals, as well as self-sampling PCR tests through the Group's specialised partners.
