Club ATITHYA


For over three decades, Club ATITHYA has been one of India's most transparent, adaptable, and easy-to-use loyalty programmes. As a Club ATITHYA member, you'll have access to a variety of exclusive member benefits at our hotels across the Globe, including award-winning restaurants and revitalising wellness centres for memorable experiences, as well as contactless redemption for hotel experiences such as Reward Nights, spa, laundry, dining, and more. Members of Club ATITHYA gain access to a wide range of benefits that accelerate as they move up tiers, including free stays, room upgrades, dining experiences, spa therapies, and more. Club ATITHYA was awarded for the best redemption ability and best customer service in 2020, as well as the Best Loyalty Programme at the 2019 Travel + Leisure Awards.

  • Contactless Redemption

    Using your mobile phone, redeem your Green Points for last-minute vacations, staycations, fine dining experiences, spa getaways, and more.Members can transfer/ gift Green Points to other Club ATITHYA membership accounts.

  • E-Vouchers

    Members that achieve/retain higher tier status receive a slew of e-vouchers, including discounts on spa and dining experiences, accommodation upgrades, and more. Value e-vouchers can be redeemed digitally by members.

  • Points never expire

    Green Points never expire for active members.Receive complementary Wi-Fi for your stays at participating hotels.The more you stay, the faster you move up the tiers & benefits multiply. Each spend and room night counts!
