ABOUT
US

What started with small steps in 2011, Atithya hotels have emerged today as a globally reputed hotel chain.

Our Hotels


From our starting location in the heart of Maharashtra - Mumbai, India; we developed our hotel chains all across the world. Atithya stands proud as a global hotel chain with hotel chains in total of 10 countires worldwide. We've given rest to lakhs of guests, employment to thousands of staff, and helped a lot of needy people along the way.


Keeping pace with the ever increasing travelling trend we have taken major initiatives in terms of stay and comfort. Innovation is the key to progress in the modern world, and our pursuit of excellence, has led to constant upgrade and adding newer areas of comfort techniques and research to make our stay more relevant. Harboring a sense of openness, participation and fairness has helped Atithya build a culture that is conducive to growth.
