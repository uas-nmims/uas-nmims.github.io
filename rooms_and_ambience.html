ROOMS AND
AMBIENCE

Adorned with elements of art and exquisite Indian motifs, our cherished Deluxe Rooms offer calming views of the lush cityscape. Unwind amidst sheer comfort and sophistication whether you’re on holiday or on business.

Atithya Rooms


We offer excellent choices of rooms with lot of variety to satisfy your needs. Our rooms have comfortable beds, in-room WiFi routers, 4K HDR Televisions, Alarm clock, bathtubs, free snack counter, mirrors, smart mood lightings, motorized curtains, voice activated commands, wireless telephones, announcement systems. As much as comfort, we focus on safety equally. Hence, each room has fire and smoke sensors, and immediate water sprinkler system to extinguish fires. There’s also an SOS button placed beside each side of bed with double tap activation system for any sort of emergency. Our hotel has numerous CCTV cameras placed all around corridors and lobbies with night vision and motion detection to keep the burglars away.
